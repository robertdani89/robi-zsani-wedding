App: Event Companion

This Privacy Policy explains how the Event Companion mobile app collects, uses, and protects personal data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Basic profile information (for example, your name).
  • Answers you submit in event questions and forms.
  • Photos you choose to upload in the app.
  • Song request information you submit.

2. Permissions We Use

  • Camera permission: used so you can take and upload photos from inside the app.
  • Media/storage permissions: used so you can select and upload existing photos.
  • Internet permission: used to send your content to our backend service.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app features for event participation.
  • To display and manage guest content (answers, photos, song requests).
  • To maintain app reliability and prevent abuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers that host the app backend and storage, solely to provide app functionality.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only for as long as needed to operate the app and related event administration, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Children

This app is not intended for independent use by children under the age required by applicable law.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.
